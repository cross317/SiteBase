About SiteBase

SiteBase is a content management system designed to give value to every idea,
by letting you create and customize your own website for free.

All you have to do is to go to the Create
page, choose your preferred style, theme, and colors, and SiteBase will generate the
website scripts for you and if you want more instructions go to the tutorial page.

Why choose SiteBase to create a website?

It is free. You can create as many websites as you want for free!
It is simple. You only have to choose a style and colors, then you change the images and the content.
It is fast. Once you complete your customization you will receive the scripts in a few seconds or minutes.
After this process you are completely free to modify your website in any way you want.
